Hi - I'm Ashley

I am a Full Stack Web Developer.
I built my first website last year while learning graphic design, and realized my passion for coding during that project. I quickly switched my focus, and recently graduated from the University of Denver Full Stack coding Bootcamp. I enjoy making beautiful applications on the front end, and the complexity and challenges of the back end.
My skills include HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, jQuery, Firebase, React.js, Mongodb, Express, SQL, and GIT. I look forward to expanding this list as I learn more.
While not online, I enjoy running trails in the high country, friendly competition at the CrossFit gym, and camping under the stars in the summer.
